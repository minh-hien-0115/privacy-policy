Data Deletion Guide

This guide explains how users can request the deletion of their personal data from our system.

1. Data Deletion Process

If you wish to delete your personal data, you can submit a request by emailing us at support@example.com. Please include the following details:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address associated with the account
  • A brief description of your request

2. Processing Time

We will process your data deletion request within 30 days of receiving your email.

3. What Happens After Deletion

Once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. You will lose access to your account and any associated data.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, please contact us at support@example.com.
